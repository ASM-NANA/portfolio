01 — Who I Am

Background &
Expertise

I hold a degree in Electrical Engineering — Electrotechnics option, with hands-on experience in power electronics, motor drives, and embedded control systems. My recent projects have pushed me into the exciting frontier of mechatronics — blending mechanical, electrical, and software intelligence into unified systems.

My ambition is to pursue a Master's in AI Applied to Industrial Automation, building on my engineering foundation to develop autonomous, data-driven industrial solutions. I am particularly interested in machine learning for predictive maintenance, computer vision for quality control, and intelligent control for smart manufacturing.

I thrive at the intersection of theory and practice — prototyping systems that are both mathematically rigorous and physically deployable in real industrial environments.
